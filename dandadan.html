Dandadan
is a fast-paced supernatural anime that follows
Momo Ayase
and
Ken Takakura (Okarun)
as they get caught between aliens, ghosts, and bizarre otherworldly threats. After a strange encounter leaves them with swapped abilities—Momo gains psychic powers while Okarun inherits a cursed turbo speed—the two team up to battle bizarre creatures and uncover mysterious forces. Blending action, comedy, romance, and chaotic paranormal energy,
Dandadan
delivers wild fights, quirky characters, and nonstop excitement as Momo and Okarun fight to protect each other and the world from the unknown.
